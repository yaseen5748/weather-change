All about Winter

Winter is the most frigid season of all time. It is one of the six seasons that come to pass in India. This season typically begins from December and lasts till February. During winter season in India, the days are smaller and the nights become more lasting. In the course of this period, the temperature, humidity, and rainfall are low. During this season, the rays of the Sun fall vertically over the Southern Hemisphere, India gets oblique rays of the Sun. The sky is clear. It is cool in the north and warm in the south. January is the coldest month. The hilly areas of Kashmir and Himachal Pradesh record the lowest temperature. Dras near Kargil has recorded the lowest temperature (-60°C). Dew and fogs are common. There is heavy snowfall in the mountain areas. India gets only 2% of its annual rainfall during winter season. There is heavy fog occasionally early in the mornings. Thus, people love travelling in India during this season.

Climate:- Cold & hazy
Temperature (Avg):- 15 – 25 °C (varies across India)
Clothing:- Thick woolen clothes
